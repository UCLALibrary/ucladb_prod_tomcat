WebVoyáge Help Purchase Request 

You can submit a purchase request to your library's administration. This purchase request form can be customized by your library, so exactly what the request may consist of depends on your library's policies. You must be logged in to place a purchase request.

To place a purchase request:

  1. Search for an item.

  2. View the Record Display of an item.

  3. Click the Make a Request link on the right side of the screen. If you have not previously logged in, the Login screen displays. Enter your bar code and last name in the appropriate fields and then click the Login button.

  4. Select the desired blank form request from the list.

  5. Enter the request information into the appropriate fields.

  6. Click the Submit button to submit the purchase order request.
