WebVoyáge Help Email Results 

Enter your email address and select a format to email your results.

To Email the displayed catalog record:
  1. Enter a valid Email address in the blank box provided next to the To: field
    (if this box is left blank or an invalid email address is entered, an error message will appear)    .
  2. Enter an email subject or use the default
  3. Enter a message in the Body Text: field provided
    (optional) This text will preceded the record in the email body
  4. Select the E-mail button
  5. A message displays confirming the search results have been mailed.
