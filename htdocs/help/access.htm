Access Restrictions to eResources

There are three types of access restrictions to eResources. The type of access available depends upon the resource. Some resources do not have restrictions, while others require you to be a UCLA Faculty, Staff or Student in order to be eligible for r emote access.

(Most remote access restricted resources are available for patrons who are trying to access from within the UCLA Library via public workstations)

Three Types of Access Restrictions to eResources
1. No restrictions No restrictions. Free resource available to general public. No individual or campus subscription necessary for access.
2. UCLA Subscription Available to UCLA faculty, staff and students only through subscriptions paid for by the UCLA Library. Accessible from computers with UCLA IP addresses only. Users connecting to the internet through commercial service providers must use the Bruin OnLine proxy server to access these resources.
3. California Digital Library Subscription Available to UC faculty, staff and students only through licenses and subcriptions paid for by the University of California. Accessible from computers with UC campus IP addresses only. Users connecting to the internet through commercial service providers must use the Bruin OnLine proxy server to access these resources.
